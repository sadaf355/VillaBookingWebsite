Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your most common questions about booking a villa with Saboo's Retreat.

Booking is simple! Browse our villa listings on the Staycations page, choose your preferred destination, and fill out the inquiry form. Our team will confirm availability and share a booking link within 2 hours. You can also call or WhatsApp us directly for same-day bookings.

All our villas come with a private pool, Wi-Fi, fully equipped kitchen, housekeeping, and parking. Many also offer premium add-ons such as in-house chef, spa services, home theatre, and outdoor activities. Specific amenities are listed on each villa's page.

Yes. We offer a flexible cancellation policy. Cancellations made 7+ days before check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations within 3–7 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours of check-in are non-refundable. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Pet policies vary by villa. Some of our properties are pet-friendly, while others are not. Please mention your pets in the inquiry form and we will match you with a suitable pet-friendly villa. A refundable pet deposit may be required.
