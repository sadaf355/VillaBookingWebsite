About Outpost Resort Alibaug:


Cherish a peaceful holiday to Alibaug, situated in India's exciting Gateways. Outpost Resort Alibaug is renowned for its spectacular scenery in the Tropical Forest. How to Reach Outpost Resort Alibaug?

The Outpost Alibaug is located 6 km from Kihim Beach. You can easily get public or private transport to the location.

Nearest Airport: Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai at a distance of about 130 km from Outpost Resort, Alibaug.

Nearest Key Locations: Kihim Beach is just 6 km away while Kolaba Fort and Kanakeshwar Forest are about 10 km from the property.

ATTRACTIONS

Alibag Top Attractions

Situated along the beautiful Arabian Sea, the popular Nagaon Beach happens to be a clean packed with suru, betel, and palm trees.The silky golden sand of this beach offers an amazing place for visitors to relax, sunbathe, and enjoy interesting beach games

Nagaon Beach

Guarded by the lush green hills, Alibaug Beach is a serene stretch of black sands edging the playfully rolling waves of the clear blue Arabian Sea. It is considered to be the primary beach of the town and hence one should definitely keep it in the list of attractions they will visit.

Alibaug Beach

Located off the coast of the Murud village near the Arabian Sea, Murud-Janjira fort is one of the strongest and beautiful marine forts of India. Around 55 km from the popular town of Alibag in Raigad district, Murud-Janjira fort is a very popular historic fort.

Murud-Janjira Fort

Encircled by the clear blue waters of the Arabian sea and offering some of the most gorgeous views around, Kolaba fort is a historical building located in Alibaug. The fort is nearly 300 years old,and had a geographical importance for the Marathas when it came to warfare.

Kolaba Fort

Just 125 kms outside of Mumbai and 33 kms from Alibaug, Kashid Beach is a popular tourist destination in Maharashtra. This remains one of the few beaches in the country that has not lost its beauty even with a high number of visitors.

Kashid Beach

Brimming with lush coconut trees, green leaves, and wildflowers, Kihim Beach is one of the most alluring tourist destinations in Maharashtra. Situated in the Kihim village of Alibaug, the beach is known for its exquisite beauty and pollution-free atmosphere.

Kihim Beach
